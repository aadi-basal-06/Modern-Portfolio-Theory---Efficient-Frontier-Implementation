🧮 THE 3 KEY FORMULAS (Linear Algebra):

   1. Portfolio Return:   R_p = w · μ    (dot product)
   2. Portfolio Risk:     σ_p = √(w^T Σ w)  (quadratic form)
   3. Sharpe Ratio:       SR = (R - Rf) / σ  (risk-adjusted return)
